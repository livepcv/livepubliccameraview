-RTSP information

--V What is RTSP V

RTSP (Real-Time Streaming Protocol) is a network protocol used for controlling and
delivering real-time multimedia content over IP networks. It enables clients to establish
sessions with streaming media servers and display a live feed over the internet
from a webcam or CCTV Camera on a webpage.

--V For Hikvision Cameras V

rtsp:(username):(password)@hostname:554/Streaming/channels/101/picture
rtsp:admin:Hello321?@mystreetcam3.hopto.org:554/Streaming/channels/101/picture

Username: admin | Password: Hello321? | Hostname: mystreetcam3.hopto.org |
RTSP Port: 554 | video feed from camera 1 /101/, using the main stream /101/,

The hostname "hopto.org" is provided by No-Ip, https://noip.com
